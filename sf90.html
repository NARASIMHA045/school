Ferrari SF90 Stradale

Specifications

  • Engine: 4.0L V8 Twin-Turbocharged
  • Power: 1000 cv (986 hp)
  • 0-100 km/h: 2.5 seconds
  • Top Speed: 340 km/h (211 mph)
  • Hybrid System: Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV)

About the SF90 Stradale

The Ferrari SF90 Stradale is a remarkable blend of performance and technology, representing the pinnacle of Ferrari's engineering. With its hybrid powertrain, it delivers exhilarating speed and efficiency. The SF90 Stradale is designed to provide an unmatched driving experience, featuring cutting-edge aerodynamics and a luxurious interior.
