Ferrari Roma

Specifications

  • Engine: 3.9L V8 Twin-Turbocharged
  • Power: 620 cv (611 hp)
  • 0-100 km/h: 3.4 seconds
  • Top Speed: 320 km/h (199 mph)
  • Transmission: 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

About the Ferrari Roma

The Ferrari Roma is a stunning grand tourer that combines elegance with performance. Inspired by the iconic designs of the past, the Roma features a sleek silhouette and a luxurious interior. With its powerful V8 engine and advanced technology, it offers an exhilarating driving experience while maintaining comfort for long journeys.
