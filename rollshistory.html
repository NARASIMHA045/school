Founding

Rolls Royce was founded in 1904 by Charles Rolls and Henry Royce. The company quickly gained a reputation for producing high-quality automobiles, with a focus on engineering excellence and luxury.

Key Milestones

  • 1906: The Rolls Royce 40/50 HP, known as the "Silver Ghost," is introduced and becomes an instant success.
  • 1925: Rolls Royce acquires the Bentley brand, expanding its luxury offerings.
  • 1931: The introduction of the Rolls Royce Phantom II sets new standards in luxury and performance.
  • 1950s: The Silver Cloud series is launched, further solidifying the brand's status in the luxury market.
  • 2003: The Rolls Royce Phantom is reintroduced, showcasing modern luxury with classic design elements.

Modern Era

Today, Rolls Royce continues to innovate while maintaining its commitment to craftsmanship. The brand is known for its bespoke vehicles, allowing customers to personalize their cars to an extraordinary degree.
