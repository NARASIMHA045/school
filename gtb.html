Ferrari 488 GTB

Introduction

The Ferrari 488 GTB is a mid-engine sports car produced by the Italian manufacturer Ferrari. It was unveiled at the 2015 Geneva Motor Show and is known for its stunning design and exceptional performance.

Image

Specifications

  • Engine: 3.9L twin-turbocharged V8
  • Power: 661 hp
  • 0-60 mph: 3.0 seconds
  • Top Speed: 205 mph
  • Transmission: 7-speed dual-clutch automatic

Features

  • Advanced aerodynamics for improved performance
  • Carbon fiber body for lightweight construction
  • High-performance braking system
  • Luxurious interior with premium materials
  • Infotainment system with navigation and connectivity options
